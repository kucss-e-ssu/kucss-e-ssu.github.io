What is GGrobee?

'GGrobee’ is a character born from the Khmer word ‘Kroap’, which means seed.

Crop is a seed character created to prevent the natural environment from deteriorating and make Cambodia a clean country.

In the process of growing, Crop goes through various obstacles and trials across four stages: waste, air pollution, water pollution, and sustainable development.

If Crop solves the problems that hinder him as he grows, he will eventually grow into something wonderful and protect the Earth's environment.

Would we all join in solving environmental problems so that Crop can grow into a wonderful person and achieve the goal of creating a clean planet?
