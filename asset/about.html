What is Krobee

"Hello guys! My name is Krobee!"

'Krobee' is a character born from the Khmer word ‘Kroap’, which means seed.

Krobee is a seed character. And Krobee strives to prevent environmental destruction and make Cambodia clean.

In the process of growing, Krobee goes through various obstacles and trials across four stages: waste problem, air pollution, water pollution, and sustainable development.

If Krobee solves the problems that prevent GGrobee’s growth, Krobee will eventually grow into something wonderful and protect the Earth's environment.

Would you join in solving environmental problems so that Krobee can thrive and achieve the goal of making the planet clean?
