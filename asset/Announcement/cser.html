Hosanna campaign "CSER"

Date: 2023-10-18
We are conducting a CSER program for reducing conserve, share, exchange, reuse in Hosanna School enviornmental class.
1. What is CSER?

CSER stands for Conserve, Share, Exchange, Reuse and refers to the activity of reducing waste by reselling unused items.

2. Announcement

Bring your unused items to sell to your next environmental class
