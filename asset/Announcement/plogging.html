Hosanna Plogging challenge "Plogging Event"

Date: 2023-10-18
We are conducting a plogging campaign to reduce waste in Hosanna School and surrounding villages.
1. What is plogging?

Plogging is a compound word of 'Plocka upp', which means 'to pick up' in Swedish, and 'Jogging', which means 'to run' in English, and refers to the activity of picking up waste around you while moving.

1. Date : From waste environment class to the next environmental class
2. How to plogging ?

Use a bag and tongs or gloves to pick up trash around town or around the school.

Take a photo of the bag you picked up the trash in and verify it using one of three methods: Instagram, Facebook, or text message. When authenticating, you must write down your grade and name and send it to us for authentication. When authenticating multiple times, authenticate to the same location as the last time.
